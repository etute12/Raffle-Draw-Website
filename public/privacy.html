Privacy and Policy

At Covenant University Raffle Draw (CURD), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you participate in our raffle draws.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Payment Information: Payment details for ticket purchases (processed securely via third-party payment gateways).
  • Participation Data: Information related to your participation in the raffle draw.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your raffle ticket purchases and manage your participation.
  • To communicate with you about the raffle draw, including updates and results.
  • To improve our services and ensure a seamless user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., payment gateways) to facilitate the raffle draw. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us at privacy@curd.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

By participating in the Covenant University Raffle Draw (CURD), you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
